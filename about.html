Mission

Technology for everyone, everywhere

EduTech was founded on a simple belief: everyone deserves access to reliable tech support, regardless of their age, income, or location. Too many people — especially elderly individuals and those in underserved communities — struggle with technology and have nowhere affordable to turn.

At the same time, millions of young people with technical skills have no pathway to gainful employment. We saw a chance to solve both problems at once by building an ecosystem that connects the people who need help with the people who can provide it.

Our model is self-sustaining: federal grants, sponsor partnerships with IT certification companies, and community donations keep our core services free while subscription plans fund ongoing growth and development.
