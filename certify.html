For Students

Launch your
IT career today

High school students earn globally recognized IT certifications and connect with real paying clients in their area. Real skills. Real income.

The Path

How to get certified

01

Enroll Through Your School

EduTech partners with high schools worldwide. Ask your IT teacher about the program, or apply directly through our platform. No prior experience required.

02

Complete Online Coursework

Work through our self-paced certification curriculum covering networking, hardware, troubleshooting, and remote support. Learn at your own pace with built-in AI support.

03

Pass the Certification Exam

Complete our practical assessment and receive your EduTech IT Certification — a globally recognized credential backed by our partner IT companies.

04

Get Matched with Clients

Once certified, you're listed on the EduTech platform. Clients in your local area can request your help for in-person tech support, and you earn real income.
